Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ContactDedup handles your information.

Information We Don't Collect

ContactDedup is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or contact data to external servers.

On-Device Processing

All contact processing, including:

  • Duplicate detection
  • Contact merging
  • Data import and export

happens entirely on your device. Your contact information never leaves your iPhone or iPad.

Third-Party Services

Apple Contacts

ContactDedup integrates with Apple's Contacts framework to read and modify your contacts. This data is managed by Apple and subject to their privacy policy.

Google Contacts

If you choose to import contacts from Google, ContactDedup uses Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication. We only request access to read your contacts. Your Google credentials are never stored by ContactDedup - authentication is handled entirely by Google's secure sign-in flow.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn import works by reading CSV files you export from LinkedIn. ContactDedup does not connect to LinkedIn directly and has no access to your LinkedIn account.

Data Storage

ContactDedup stores minimal app preferences (such as your duplicate detection sensitivity settings) locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. No contact data is stored separately from Apple's Contacts database.

Analytics

ContactDedup does not include any analytics or tracking software. We do not track how you use the app or collect any usage statistics.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
